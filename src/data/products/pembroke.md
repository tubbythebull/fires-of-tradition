This distinguished combination front features the same beloved Victorian detailing—graceful vine scrollwork, delicate beaded trim, and a grand arched opening—now proportioned to anchor larger spaces with authoritative elegance. Ideal for principal rooms, formal parlors, dining halls, and generous living areas, the Pembroke transforms substantial interiors into showcases of period craftsmanship and timeless design mastery.

The Pembroke takes the refined aesthetics of Victorian fireplace design and reimagines them at a bolder scale. ## Installation & Use

### Electric Fireplace Configuration

Building codes and the absence of chimney systems need not prevent the installation of a traditional hearth in larger rooms. Our refined electric firebox offerings install throughout your residence, delivering both operational safety and impressive visual realism that honors Victorian authenticity.

### Flexible Placement Options

Compatible with our electric firebox selections for installations where venting proves impractical. With no chimney required, the Pembroke excels in renovated spaces, new construction, upper floors, and interior rooms where traditional masonry systems are absent or impractical.

### Traditional Fireplace Applications

Our castings integrate beautifully with renovated wood-burning fireplaces and properly sized masonry fireboxes, preserving authentic Victorian character while enabling traditional wood burning or, with appropriate professional installation and local code compliance, natural gas operation.

## Materials, Care & Maintenance

### Material Composition

Authentic cast iron construction with period-inspired finishes that capture the aged patina and dignified character of treasured antiques.

### Finish Options

We present these castings in two heritage-inspired finishes: Antique Iron Lustre and Black Burnished Bronze. Both finishes possess subtle reflective qualities that capture and interact with ambient light across the raised ornamental details, creating dimensional depth and visual intrigue that enhances the commanding presence of this wider casting.

### Care Requirements

Regular dusting maintains appearance; avoid soap, water, and harsh abrasives that may compromise the finish integrity.

### Safety Protocols

All wood-burning and gas installations must adhere to local building regulations, appropriate hearth construction, proper firebox sizing, and clearance requirements. Electric configurations should follow manufacturer specifications for firebox installation and operational safety.
